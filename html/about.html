Our history

In 2015, with little capital but a pocketful of belief our CEO, Alauddin Kaikaus started Technophile, a software company. The new company initially focused on the international market with the local market. Since then the company has shown a continuous growth and currently employs over 100+ software engineers. Technophile is now not only an established name in Bangladesh but also in countries like the USA, UK, Netherlands, Denmark, Japan, Norway, Sweden, Germany, Canada, Switzerland, Turkey and the Middle East.

End to end quality management

Technophile has emphasized on Quality Management practices since its inception. Technophile practices world class quality management tools to ensure consistent quality of its products and processes. All activities of Technophile are monitored using a customized quality dashboard. Technophile has in-house quality auditing team who constantly monitors processes and identify improvement scopes. Technophile quality management practices start from deriving the drawing board solutions and are ensured even in after sales services, making it an end to end process.
